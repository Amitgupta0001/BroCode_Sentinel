## Solving the Identity Crisis for a Zero-Trust World

BroCode Sentinel is designed for mission-critical environments where **point-in-time authentication is a liability**. By shifting to **Continuous Verification**, we serve a diverse range of high-stakes industries.

### **1. üíº Enterprise Workspace Integrity**
*Ensuring the "Human-to-Hardware" bond in a remote-first economy.*
- **The Challenge**: Employees leaving active sessions unattended or sharing corporate laptops.
- **Sentinel Solution**: Continuous gaze and posture monitoring combined with Keystroke DNA. If a family member or stranger sits at the machine, the trust score drops below 0.3, instantly triggering a **Graduated Response** (Warning -> Challenge -> Lockout).
- **Compliance**: Fully supports GDPR/HIPAA by ensuring data is only accessed by the authorized biometric owner.

### **2. üè¶ Next-Gen Fintech & Crypto Exchanges**
*Securing the movement of wealth with Quantum-Proof Armor.*
- **The Challenge**: Session high-jacking via cookie theft or SIM swapping.
- **Sentinel Solution**: Every transaction or high-value movement is checked against the user's **Behavioral Profile**. A hacker may have the password, but they cannot replicate the user's unique typing cadence or head-to-camera motion signatures.
- **Quantum Armor**: All telemetry is signed with NIST-standard PQC (Dilithium), protecting financial data from future quantum-computer decryption.

### **3. üéì Academic Integrity & Online Proctoring**
*Scaling remote education without compromising trust.*
- **The Challenge**: Students using external aids, deepfakes, or stand-ins during exams.
- **Sentinel Solution**: **Neural Vision** tracks gaze to ensure the user is focused on the exam UI. **Adaptive Liveness** (Blink/Texture analysis) detects and blocks screen-replay attacks or deepfake software injections.
- **Scalability**: Replaces thousands of human proctors with a server-side AI engine.

### **4. üè• Healthcare Roaming Identity**
*Protecting PHI (Patient Health Information) in fast-paced clinical environments.*
- **The Challenge**: Doctors/Nurses moving between shared terminals and forgetting to log out.
- **Sentinel Solution**: **Seamless Presence Detection**. The terminal automatically stays unlocked as long as the authorized medical professional is detected. As soon as they walk away, the screen locks. **Identity-as-a-Service** without the friction of keycards or passwords.
